Who Am I?

I’m Righteous, a passionate and detail-oriented Frontend Web Developer who loves turning designs into functional, accessible, and responsive websites.

My journey into tech started with a fascination for how websites worked. What began as curiosity quickly became a mission to create engaging user experiences using HTML, CSS, and modern frontend frameworks. I'm driven by a constant desire to learn, build, and improve.

“Keep building, keep growing.” That’s the mindset that fuels me daily.

I’m not just focused on making things look great—I’m also committed to clean code, performance, and user-first design. I see every project as a chance to grow both technically and creatively.

How it all started...

It all began during the lockdown. I came across a video explaining how websites are built, and I was instantly hooked. I didn’t have a tech background, but the idea of building something from scratch with code fascinated me.

- A spark turned into purpose.

I started teaching myself using YouTube and free online resources. Step by step, I began to understand HTML, CSS, and the power of design systems. Since then, I’ve been committed to this journey—building, breaking, and rebuilding to get better each day.

Future plans

  • I’m currently enrolled in several frontend and UI-focused courses to sharpen my skills further. I’m advancing in React, TypeScript, and design systems through Zero To Mastery Academy.
  • I’m also working on building my own component library and contributing to open-source design assets. Giving back to the community is something I value deeply.
  • At the moment, I’m handling two ongoing projects. One is a UI-heavy startup landing page, and the other is a full portfolio template for junior developers to customize and use freely.
  • One of my proudest accomplishments so far is designing a clean, responsive dashboard template. Through this, I learned key concepts like state management, responsive grid systems, accessibility standards, and performance optimization.

    • I strongly believe in continuous improvement, learning from others, staying curious, and embracing challenges.
